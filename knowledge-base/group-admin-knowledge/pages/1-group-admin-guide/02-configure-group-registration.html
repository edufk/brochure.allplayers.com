Configure Group Registration

Group registration allows groups to register for events such as tournaments and leagues, rather than the league creating all of the teams.

This guide assumes that you already have individual registration set up for the group, if you you still need to set up individual registration, click here.

Enable group registration

Navigate to the group that you would like members to join.

Select Administrative Tools > Registration in your Group Toolbox.


Click here to learn more about the group toolbox.


Click the Group Registration tab.


Click the On/Off switch to enable group registration.

Configure group registration settings

Role to assign to the registering group admin

Select a role to assign to the group admin of the registering group.

If no role is assigned, the admin will need to select the correct role during the registration process.

Charge registration fees

Charge a registration fee to members of the registering group.

  • Bulk fee(s) from the group: Charge a flat fee for registering a group, regardless of how many members are registering and their roles.
  • Fees from the assigned role: Charge a fee for each user according to the role that they are being registered for.
  • No fees: Do not charge a fee for group registration.

This option will only be visible if the group is set up to charge registration fees. Click here to learn how to accept payments for your group.

Group registration fees

Select a fee to charge to registrants.

This option will only be visible if the group is set up to charge registration fees and the Charge registration fees option is set to Bulk fee(s) from the group.

Charge registration fees to

Select who to charge registration fees to.

  • Let the registering group admin decide: During registration, the group admin will decide whether they will pay the fee, or if it will be charged to the individual group members.
  • The registering group admin: Charge the fee to the registering group admin.
  • Each registered member: Charge the fee to each individual group member. If Bulk fee(s) from the group is the chosen option for Charge registration fees, the fee will be split evenly between the registering members.

This option will only be visible if the group is set up to charge registration fees and the Charge registration fees option is set to Bulk fee(s) from the group or Fees from the assigned role.

Forms at registration

Choose whether or not the registering group admin can skip filling out webforms. The individual group members will still have to fill out the forms if the group admin chooses to skip filling them out.

Automatically enable registration for subgroups

If this option is enabled, the registering group will have its individual registration automatically enabled with the same role settings chosen in this group's group registration.

Maximum registration groups

Limit the number of groups that can be registered to this group.

Group role limits

Limit the number of people that can register with a chosen role for each group registered.

Configure group role settings

Registration Forms

If your group needs to collect data from your members as they register for your group, you can Add a Registration Form to request this information. If your group is related to other groups that already have registration forms, you can choose a preexisting form.

Age Requirements

If a role is age-restricted, choose the minimum and maximum ages. TIP: If your league allows players to "play up" to the next age level, ensure that your minimum age is set to allow younger players to join the group.

Gender Requirements

Restrict your role to allow only males or females in a given role (for example, players in a girls' basketball league) if necessary.

Registration Fees

If your group needs to collect a registration fee from its registrants, you can add a registration fee. If your group is related to other groups that already have registration fees, you can choose a preexisting fee.

This option will only be visible if the group is set up to charge registration fees and the Charge registration fees option is set to Fees from the assigned role.

Skip role selection

Enabling this setting will skip the role selection page.

Warning: This should only be used when there is only one role available with no fee attached.

Hide role selection

If you do not want to show a role on your registration page for your group, check this box. Then provide the Direct Role Registration URL to the person(s) you want to register for this role.

Challenge Code

If you only want certain people to register for a specific role in your group, enable the challenge code and enter a code of your choice. Provide this code to a user to restrict registration to only those who know the code. The role will only become visible once the challenge code is entered.

Allow when registering others

For current group members who have the permission to register other users to this group, this role can only be added by them if this option is enabled.

Flag role when paid

For roles with registration fees attached, assign the specified flag to the user when they have completed payment.

Additional role when payment complete

For roles with registration fees attached, assign an additional role to the registrant once they have completed payment.

Do not assign this role until fees are paid

This role will not be assigned to the registrant until the registration fee is paid.

Save Settings

Once you are finished setting up your roles, click Save configuration.
