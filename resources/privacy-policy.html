PRIVACY POLICY

Our privacy commitment to you

AllPlayers.com is an open-source Platform-As-A-Service (PaaS) solution that seamlessly links organizations and their membership together in one unified environment. Two of the many features that most organizations utilize within the AllPlayers.com platform are our user registration service and member management solution. When organizations use the AllPlayers.com platform to connect with their membership it creates both value and convenience for the organization and their members. Organizations have an enterprise cloud-based solution with all the security and stability necessary to handle groups of any size, and their members have real-time access to information from the website or through our mobile apps.

We appreciate the trust that organizations place in AllPlayers.com, Inc. and are committed to respecting the privacy and security of any information stored, collected and retrieved through their membership management solution. The AllPlayers.com platform only requires the following fields to create an account and will never collect more personal information than is reasonably necessary to participate in a program or activity:

  • First Name
  • Last Name
  • Email
  • Birthdate
  • Gender

Any other information collected or requested is organization specific and only accessible by that organization and you, and is not shared, distributed, sold or used for any marketing activity by AllPlayers.com. We do not disclose any personal information to third parties. As with any website there is data that is collected and stored on your personal electronic device (computer, laptop, tablet, pad, smart phone, android, etc.) while you are utilizing the platform. The information below describes the data and how AllPlayers.com uses the information.

Information we collect

When you visit AllPlayers.com, you provide us with personal information that you knowingly choose to disclose through registration and website use, and is collected by us as you interact with our website and enter information and data.

AllPlayers.com gathers information about how visitors navigate through our website by using clickstream data gathered with cookies, a data file that is stored by the web browser on your computer, and / or other online tools. Cookies enable you to be recognized when you return to our website to review information, add / update / delete information, or place an order. They also allow us to enhance and personalize your online experience, so that the information you receive is more relevant to you.

As part of any team registration fee collection, fundraising donation, and product or service transaction, we may collect information necessary to process your requested action, including transaction and credit card information. No credit card data is exposed, viewable or stored on the AllPlayers.com website. This information is passed directly to your organization’s merchant processor, and stored in their secure facility. You can remain confident that the only additional information asked for will be used solely to complete and process the transaction.

Any improper collection or misuse of information provided on AllPlayers.com is a violation of the terms of use and should be reported to customer support.

Using information we collect

Profile and personal information is entered by you and is used by AllPlayers.com primarily to be presented back to you and edited by you when you access the service. You maintain control of your personal information. When you register for a group, the information collected during registration is presented to the appropriate organization within our member management solution, which you control through your privacy settings. In some cases where your privacy settings permit it (e.g., posting a comment to your profile page feature), other AllPlayers.com users may be able to add information to your profile, but you will always maintain control of your profile through your privacy settings and site features (e.g., deleting a posted comment feature). At AllPlayers.com, we strive to provide you with the best possible online experience and manage your transactions exactly as you've requested. In order to do this, we collect information that allows us to:

  • Process and track your transaction
  • Provide the services that you request and process payments in conjunction with those requests
  • Contact you about the status of a transaction
  • Improve our merchandise selection and customer service level

Information we share with others and your privacy rights

AllPlayers.com, Inc. contracts with other companies to provide certain services, including credit card processing, shipping, and name and address verification. We provide these companies with only the information they need to perform their services and work closely with them to ensure that your privacy is respected and protected. These companies are prohibited by contract, signed by your organization, from using this information for their own marketing purposes or from sharing this information with anyone other than AllPlayers.com.

On rare occasions, we may disclose specific information upon governmental request, in response to a court order, or when required by law to do so. We may also share information with companies assisting in fraud protection or investigation. We do not provide information to these agencies or companies for marketing or commercial purposes.

Security

It is our intent to protect against the loss, misuse or alteration of information that we have collected from you or received from a partner. We use a variety of current technologies and processes for protection of our customer data. Examples of the AllPlayers.com security and privacy measures include: physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards; security monitoring tools; documented security policies; use of strong encryption for transmissions of sensitive data; and restricted access of personally identifiable information.

When you create an account or make a purchase on our website, your transactional information is transmitted in a safe, encrypted format. AllPlayers.com is committed to privacy and security, and as such, is compliant with the Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard ("PCI DSS") and has partnered with a Level 1 service provider for credit card transactions.

Privacy of children age 12 and under on our website – COPPA Compliance

At AllPlayers.com, we are committed to providing additional privacy protection for children who visit our site.

The Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), passed by Congress in October, 1998 requires the Federal Trade Commission (FTC) to issue and enforce rules concerning children’s online privacy. The primary goal is to place parents in control over what information is collected from their child online.

AllPlayers.com is very serious about total compliance with COPPA and has put the following provisions in place to ensure that every child’s privacy is protected. COPPA defines a child as their age being 12 and under.

  • AllPlayers.com is committed to and is compliant with the federal Child Online Privacy and Protection Act (COPPA – http://www.ftc.gov/ogc/coppa1.htm).
  • Children age 12 and under are strictly prohibited from creating an account on AllPlayers.com and are not allowed to create an account on the AllPlayers.com site.
  • All personal information regarding children age 12 and under is collected actively from either the parent or legal guardian and is used to create an account. The personal information is limited to the name, birth date, gender and email address.
  • Parents or legal guardians of children age 12 and under are the only ones who have the ability to create an account, review the account and manage the online environment for their children age 12 and under on AllPlayers.com.
  • Children age 12 and under cannot enter any personal identifiable information on the AllPlayers.com website without their parent's or legal guardian’s expressed permission and consent.
  • The user accounts of any child age 12 and under that are created by parents or legal guardians are not included in the AllPlayers.com search engine results and can only be accessed according to the privacy settings configured by the parent or legal guardian; and can only be viewed by family members or “group mates”.
  • AllPlayers.com does not share or disclose any personal information with third parties.
  • Parents or legal guardians of children age 12 and under can have their child’s personal information deleted from AllPlayers.com at any time by contacting the AllPlayers.com Support team. Direct Link: support.allplayers.com
